Course List

Below is a curated list of courses that we offer. We do suggest you take our courses in tandem but can be taken separately if you already have an understanding of what's covered

We hope you enjoy our content and as always, happy brewing!

Course Topic: History of Coffee (the good and the bad)

In this course, Phil goes over the historic the origins of coffee and how it's transformed into a modern artform as we know it. We will also be going over how coffee companies have capitalized on human addiction to increase profits and scale their operations globally! Aren't you excited?

Course Topic: Everything Coffee Bean

In this course, Phil goes over where and how coffee beans are grown and sourced. From baby coffee bean cherry sprouting from soft soil, to being roasted and served at a starbucks near you. We will be looking at how the shape, texture, feel and roasting process all affect the notes of the coffee when brewed and poured.

Course Topic: A Latte Art

In this course, Phil teaches you how to give your latte's the artistic design that will have your customers flooding at the gates. In this finale, Phil teaches you how to steam and pour your milk for a different variety of coffees. Whether it's your casual cappicino or latte lover, at the end of this course you will know how to make milk art for any given request!
